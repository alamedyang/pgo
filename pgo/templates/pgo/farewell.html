As many of you already know, I completely stopped playing PGo almost two years ago. Had a little comeback when PvP was released, but that didn't last and I've since moved on to other games, projects, and activities.

Despite this I tried to keep the calculator updated for as long as possible, but recently other commercial and hobby projects have taken priority. The only time I had left to work on the breakpoint calc was time I'd rather spend with friends and family or sleeping.

I never expected the breakpoint calc would live this long and for that I must thank all of you for using it and messaging me about game updates, but this is farewell. Maybe we'll meet again in some other game and then I might even be using your calculator :)
